In what format do I type in the performance?

Performances are typed using the official format of the IAAF. But don't worry! There is always an example in the info button info next to the "Performance" field.

question_answer
How do you calculate the points?

The points are calculated based on the latest Scoring tables of 2017 and the latest Ranking tables of 2019 that, the international athletics federation has published.

question_answer
Can I calculate the points without the app?

Sure! You can read about and use the Scoring and Ranking tables that you will find below.

question_answer
Where did you find the meetings?

The meetings along with their respective categories, are taken from the IAAF's official site. However, feel free to contact us to add any meeting missing.

question_answer
I found a mistake in your app. What do I do?

We want the app to be as reliable as possible, so contact us immediately. Use the Contact button and report your problem, even if you are not sure about it.
