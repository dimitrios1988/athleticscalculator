What meetings are in each category?
World Athletics determines to which Category a given competition should be assigned. There are 4 high ranked categories followed by 6 more categories.
  • OW - Olympic Games, World Outdoor Championships
  • DF - Diamond League Finals
  • GW - World Indoor Championships, World Cross Country / half-marathon / Race Walking Championships, Continental Cup, Diamond League Meetings
  • GL - Senior Area Championships, Hammer Throw / Combined / Race Walking Challenge, Gold Label Marathons
There are 6 more categories, named from A'-F', in which

  • Meeting category B' are considered to be the Senior National Outdoor Championships.

    Note: Foreign athletes do not receive any points from place

Which events are similar?
An indoor event that also exists in the outdoors, is considered to be its similar. Additionally, the following events are considered to have them as similars.
  • 100m - 50m, 55m, 60m
  • 400m - 300m, 300m indoor, 500m, 500m indoor
  • 800m - 600m, 600m indoor, 1000m, 1000m indoor
  • 1500m - Mile, Mile indoor, 2000m, 2000m indoor
  • 5000m - 3000m, 3000m indoor, 2 Miles, 2 Miles indoor, 5000m indoor
  • 10,000m - 5000m, Cross Country (WXC only)
  • 110mH - 50mH, 55mH, 60mH
  • 3000mSC - 2000mSC
  • Road Running - 5 km, 10 km, 15 km, 10 Miles, 20km
  • Marathon - Half Marathon, 25 km, 30 km
  • 20km Race Walking - 5km, 10km, 15km, 10,000m, 15,000m, 20,000m
  • 35km Race Walking - 20km, 30km, 35km, 20,000m, 30,000m, 50,000m. Please note that 20km Race Walking will be valid until 26/7/2022

How many competitions do I need in order to be in World Athletics' ranking?
You will need to have at least 5 competitions in total, with 3 of them being on the main event (which means that you can have up to 2 similar events out of 5 that will be counted) with a few exceptions:
  • For the 3000m steeple and the 5000m, you will need 3 competitions in total, with at least 2 of them being on the main event.
  • For the 10.000m., you will need 2 competitions in total, with at least 1 of them being on the main event.
  • For the combined events, you will need 2 competitions in total, with at least 1 of them being on the main event.
  • For the road running group (10km and half-marathon), as well as for the 20km race walking, you will need 3 competitions in total, with at least 2 of them being on the main event.
  • For the Marathon, as well as for the 50km race walking, you will need 2 competitions in total, with at least 1 of them being on the main event.

For how long are my competition's points in effect?
  • Ranking points are considered to be in effect for a 12-month period, with the exception of the combined events, the 10.000m and the road events, which will be in effect for a 18-month period.
  • The points that have been earned from OW & DF meeting categories, are considered to be in effect up until the next meeting of the same category.
  • The points that have been earned from an outdoor's area championship, are considered to be in effect up until the next one, despite the fact that the 12-month (or 18-month) period might have been completed.
